# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), version 2.1.

In short: be respectful, be constructive, and assume good faith. Behaviour that makes the project unwelcoming for others is not acceptable, regardless of who you are or what you've contributed.

The full text of the Contributor Covenant 2.1 is available at the link above and applies in full to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other channel associated with this repository.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behaviour that violates this code, please report it to the maintainers by opening a [private security advisory](https://github.com/eduardoarantes/agentenv/security/advisories/new) on this repository. Reports will be handled confidentially.

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code, and may take any action they deem appropriate, up to and including removing comments, commits, or contributors from project spaces.
